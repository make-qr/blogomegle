# Morgan Rivers

**Staff Essayist · OmegleChat**

*On loneliness, conversation, and the courage to say hello again.*

<div class="author-card">

Morgan Rivers writes serial essays about modern connection — the quiet kind, not the viral kind.

After years of writing about digital culture and social health, Morgan began **[The Quiet Hours Chronicle]({{ '/series/the-quiet-hours-chronicle/' | relative_url }})** — a first-person serial in six parts on loneliness, sadness, friendship, and love.

The chronicle is written as lived experience: not self-help slogans, but nights, messages, mistakes, and small recoveries.
