## About this serial

*Late Bloom Stories* is a **six-part fiction serial** in English — each episode is a standalone love story with a **happy ending**, tied together by one idea: some feelings wait years before they dare to open, like cherry blossoms after a long winter.

Every story touches **loneliness** and **connection** — including quiet nights when strangers online help for an hour, but the real conversation might still be offline.

**~6–10 min listen per part** · Start with Part I.
